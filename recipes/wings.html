Chicken Wings

This baked wings recipe has the most amazing seasoning consisting of paprika, garlic, salt and pepper. Simple but delicious. This Chicken Wings Recipe is a healthier and more flavorful way to cook your favorite appetizer recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Chiken wings
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt & pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Line a baking sheet with foil and dot it with butter.
  2. Combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt & pepper in a mixing bowl.
  3. Fully coat each wing with the seasoning mix and place it on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes at 425Â°F. Then turn the wings over and bake another 15 minutes.
